Welcome to Day 14 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **Strength in Surrender**.


Our Scripture comes from the Gospel of Luke, chapter 22, verse 42.

Listen to Jesus’ prayer in the garden:

“Father, if you are willing, take this cup from me; yet not my will, but yours be done.”


Surrender is not weakness or defeat.  
It is the bravest kind of trust.  
It is saying to God, “I release my plans, my control, my fears—  
and I trust Yours instead.”

In that garden, Jesus showed us the way.  
He felt the weight, the longing to avoid pain,  
yet He chose surrender—and found strength beyond His own.

God strengthens those who yield to Him.  
When you let go, He takes what you release  
and fills you with His peace, His purpose, His power.

Today, you are invited to practice that same surrender.


Here is today’s reflection:

Surrender is not defeat—it is trust.  
God strengthens those who yield to Him.


Let us pray together.  
You may speak these words slowly or hold them in your heart:

Lord,  
I surrender my plans to You.  
Lead me.  
Amen.


Now, a simple but powerful activity for today.

Find a quiet moment—perhaps right now—  
and say this prayer slowly, word by word:

“Your will, not mine.”

Repeat it a few times, letting each word sink in.  
Feel the release with each breath.  
Offer whatever you are holding onto today—  
a worry, a decision, a dream—  
and trust it into His hands.


In surrender, you are not losing.  
You are gaining His strength.  
He is faithful to carry what you give Him.

You are held.  
You are guided.  
You are strong in His love.
