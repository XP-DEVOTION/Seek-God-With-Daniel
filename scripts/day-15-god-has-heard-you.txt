Welcome to Day 15 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **God Has Heard You**.


Our Scripture comes from Daniel, chapter 10, verse 12.

Listen to these powerful words spoken by the angel to Daniel:

“From the first day that you set your mind to gain understanding, your words were heard.”


God hears you the moment you turn toward Him.  
The very first day you pray—  
the first moment you open your heart—  
your words reach His ears.  
They are received.  
They are treasured.

There may be delays in the answers you see.  
There may be battles in the unseen.  
But your prayer is never ignored,  
never forgotten,  
never too small or too late.

God is already responding.  
He is already near.

Today, you are invited to rest in this truth:  
You have been heard.


Here is today’s reflection:

God hears you the moment you turn toward Him.  
Your prayer is never ignored.


Let us pray together.  
You may speak these words softly or hold them in your heart:

Lord,  
thank You for hearing me.  
Help me trust that You are near.  
Amen.


Now, a gentle activity for today.

Find a quiet moment—perhaps right now—  
and write a short prayer to God.  
It could be a sentence,  
a word of thanks,  
a request,  
or simply “I am here.”

Write it honestly.  
Then, hold it before Him  
and trust that He receives it fully—  
right now,  
completely,  
with love.

You don’t need to say more.  
He has heard.


Rest in this assurance today.  
You are heard.  
You are loved.  
You are never alone.
