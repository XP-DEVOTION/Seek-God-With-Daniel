Welcome to Day 19 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **Understanding the Mission**.


Our Scripture comes from the Gospel of John, chapter 20, verse 21.

Listen to the words Jesus spoke to His disciples after the resurrection:

“As the Father has sent me, so I send you.”


God does not simply comfort you and then leave you untouched by the world.  
He fills you, heals you, strengthens you—  
and then He sends you.

Your life is not only for your own peace.  
It has purpose in His greater story.

You are sent—  
into your family,  
your workplace,  
your neighborhood,  
your ordinary day—  
to carry the same love, forgiveness, and presence  
that Jesus carried.

Today, you are invited to awaken to this gentle mission  
and to ask where He is sending you right now.


Here is today’s reflection:

God doesn’t just comfort you—He sends you.  
Your life has purpose in His plan.


Let us pray together.  
You may speak these words softly or hold them in your heart:

Jesus,  
show me the mission You have for me today.  
Amen.


Now, a simple activity for today.

Take a quiet moment—perhaps right now—  
and ask God this honest question:

“Where are You sending me today?”

It might be to one person,  
one conversation,  
one act of kindness,  
or simply to carry peace into a room.

Listen quietly for His gentle answer.  
It will likely be small, ordinary, and close at hand.

When it comes, receive it with an open heart  
and say yes.


You do not need to change the world today.  
You only need to be faithful in the place He sends you.  
His love flowing through you  
is enough.

You are sent—  
and you are not sent alone.
