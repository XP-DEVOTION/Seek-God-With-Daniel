Welcome to Day 13 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **The Quiet Conversion**.


Our Scripture comes from the Gospel of Mark, chapter 4, verse 27.

Listen to these simple, profound words of Jesus:

“The seed sprouts and grows, though he does not know how.”


Most of the real work of transformation happens out of sight.  
Like a seed in the dark soil,  
your heart is being changed in ways you cannot measure or control.

You may not feel different today.  
You may not see dramatic results.  
But God is at work—quietly, steadily, faithfully—  
shaping you from the inside out.

The quiet conversion is the truest kind.  
It is the kind that lasts.

Today, you are invited to trust the hidden growth  
that is already happening in you.


Here is today’s reflection:

Most transformation is quiet.  
God works beneath the surface, shaping your heart in hidden ways.


Let us pray together.  
You may speak these words softly or hold them in your heart:

God,  
thank You for the quiet ways You are changing me.  
Amen.


Now, a gentle activity for today.

Take a quiet moment—perhaps right now—  
and look back over these first twelve days.

Notice one small change in yourself.  
It might be a softer heart,  
a moment of greater patience,  
a new awareness of God’s presence,  
or simply the fact that you have kept showing up.

It doesn’t need to be big.  
Just name one small thing that feels different.

Then, quietly thank God for that hidden work.


You are being changed—  
not by your striving,  
but by His gentle, faithful hand.

Trust the quiet growth.  
It is real.
