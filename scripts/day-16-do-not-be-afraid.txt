Welcome to Day 16 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **Do Not Be Afraid**.


Our Scripture comes from Daniel, chapter 10, verse 19.

Hear the angel’s tender words to Daniel:

“Do not be afraid, you who are highly esteemed.”


Fear can feel loud and heavy.  
It tells you that you are alone,  
that the struggle is too much,  
that you are not enough.

But God sees you differently.  
He calls you highly esteemed—  
precious,  
beloved,  
worthy of His attention and care.

And into your fear, He speaks the same words He spoke to Daniel:  
Do not be afraid.

Not because the challenge disappears,  
but because His presence is stronger than the fear.

Today, you are invited to hear those words spoken over you  
and to let them quiet your heart.

Here is today’s reflection:

God speaks courage into your fear.  
His presence strengthens trembling hearts.


Let us pray together.  
You may speak these words softly or hold them in your heart:

Jesus,  
speak courage into my fear today.  
Amen.


Now, a gentle activity for today.

Take a quiet moment—perhaps right now—  
and name one fear that is present in your heart.

It could be big or small.  
Simply name it honestly before God.

Then, listen as He speaks these words over you:

“Do not be afraid—you are highly esteemed.”

Let those words cover the fear you named.  
Breathe them in slowly.


You are not defined by your fear.  
You are defined by His love.  
And His perfect love casts out fear.

You are safe.  
You are seen.  
You are deeply loved.
