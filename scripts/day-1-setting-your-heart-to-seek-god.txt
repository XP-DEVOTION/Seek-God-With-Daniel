Welcome to Day 1 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **Setting Your Heart to Seek God**.


Our Scripture comes from the book of Daniel, chapter 10, verse 2.

Listen to these words:

“I, Daniel, had been in mourning for three weeks.”


Daniel created space in his life – a space of intention, of mourning, of longing – so that he could hear God more clearly.

Today, you are invited to do the same.

This 21-day journey begins with one simple thing: a quiet yes to God.

You don’t need dramatic changes or perfect feelings.  
You only need to turn your heart toward Him, even just a little.


Here is today’s short reflection:

Today begins a 21-day journey of intention and openness.  
Daniel created space to hear God more clearly.  
You begin with the same simple yes.


Let’s pray together.

You can pray these words out loud or silently in your heart:

Lord,  
as I start these 21 days,  
turn my heart toward You.  
Quiet the noise around me  
and teach me to listen with humility and hope.  
Amen.


Now, a simple activity for today.

Find a quiet moment – perhaps right now.  
Take five slow, deep breaths.

With each breath out, whisper softly:

“Here I am, Lord.”


Inhale slowly…  
Exhale… “Here I am, Lord.”  
Again…  
Inhale…  
Exhale… “Here I am, Lord.”


Thank you for beginning this journey with an open heart.

God is already near.
