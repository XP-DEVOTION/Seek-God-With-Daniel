Welcome to Day 20 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **Standing Firm in Grace**.



Our Scripture comes from First Peter, chapter 5, verse 12.

Listen to these steadying words:

“This is the true grace of God. Stand fast in it.”



Grace is not fragile.  
It is not a thin thread that might snap when life gets hard.  
It is a firm foundation—  
solid,  
unshakable,  
able to hold you when everything around you shakes.

The storms will come.  
Doubt will whisper.  
Weariness will pull.  
But grace does not move.  
It stands.  
And it calls you to stand in it—  
not by your own effort,  
but by resting your full weight on what is already true.

Today, you are invited to plant your feet in this grace  
and stand firm.



Here is today’s reflection:

Grace is not fragile.  
It holds you steady when life shakes.



Let us pray together.  
You may speak these words softly or hold them in your heart:

Lord,  
help me stand firm in Your grace today.  
Amen.


Now, a simple but anchoring activity for today.

Whenever you feel unsteady today—  
a wave of worry,  
a moment of doubt,  
or simply the weight of the day—  
pause for one deep breath.

As you inhale, quietly receive His grace.  
As you exhale, say in your heart:

“Your grace is enough.”

Do this once, or as many times as you need.  
Let it be your quiet declaration:  
I am standing in grace.  
I will not be moved.



You have almost completed this journey.  
You are stronger than you know—  
not because you have tried harder,  
but because you have learned to stand in grace.

It is holding you.  
It will not let you go.
