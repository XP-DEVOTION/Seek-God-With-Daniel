Welcome to Day 5 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **When Desire Feels Small**.


Our Scripture comes from the Gospel of Matthew, chapter 5, verse 6.

Listen to these words:

“Blessed are those who hunger and thirst for righteousness, for they will be filled.”


Some days, your desire for God feels strong and bright.  
Other days, it feels faint—almost too small to notice.

But Jesus says even that small hunger is blessed.  
Even the faintest thirst matters to Him.

You do not need to manufacture big feelings.  
You only need to offer whatever desire you have today,  
however small it may seem.

God can work with the tiniest spark.


Here is today’s reflection:

Some days your desire for God feels small.  
Offer it anyway.  
God can work with even the faintest hunger.


Let us pray together.  
You may speak these words quietly or hold them in your heart:

Lord,  
receive my small desire.  
Increase my hunger for You.  
Amen.


Now, a simple activity for today.

At some point today—perhaps right now—  
pray this one short sentence, slowly and honestly:

“Lord, increase my desire for You.”

You can say it once, or repeat it several times throughout the day.  
Let it be your quiet offering.


That’s all He asks.  
Just bring what you have.  
He will do the rest.

You are loved exactly as you are today.
