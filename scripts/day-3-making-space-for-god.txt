Welcome to Day 3 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **Making Space for God**.


Our Scripture comes from Psalm 46, verse 10.

Listen carefully to these words:

“Be still, and know that I am God.”


Stillness is not emptiness.  
It is spaciousness.  
When you pause—when you quiet the rush—you create room  
for God to speak,  
for God to move,  
for God to simply be with you.

Today is an invitation to practice that sacred pause.


Here is today’s reflection:

Stillness is not empty—it is spacious.  
When you pause, you make room for God to speak and move.


Let us pray together.  
You may say these words aloud or hold them silently:

Lord,  
teach me to be still.  
Help me notice Your presence in the quiet moments.  
Amen.


Now, a gentle activity for today.

If you can, find a comfortable place to sit right now.  
Let your body relax.  
Let your hands rest open.

For one full minute, simply sit in silence.

Let your breathing slow naturally.  
No need to force anything.  
Just be present,  
and quietly let God be God.


Well done.  
Even this small moment of stillness is a gift you have offered to God—  
and a gift He has given back to you.
