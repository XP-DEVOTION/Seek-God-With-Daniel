Welcome to Day 4 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **The Slow Work of Grace**.


Our Scripture is from the letter of James, chapter 5, verse 7.

Listen to these words:

“Be patient, therefore, beloved, until the coming of the Lord.”


Grace does not rush.  
It works quietly, deeply, like a farmer waiting for rain,  
like seeds pushing through soil in the dark.  
You may not see the growth today,  
but God is moving—always moving—in ways you cannot yet see.

Today, you are invited to trust that slow, hidden work.



Here is today’s reflection:

Grace works slowly, like seeds growing underground.  
Trust that God is moving even when you cannot see it.



Let us pray together.  
Speak or hold these words in your heart:

God,  
give me patience with myself  
and with Your timing.  
Help me trust Your slow work.  
Amen.


Now, a gentle activity for today.

Choose one ordinary task you will do today—  
washing dishes, sending an email, walking to the car,  
anything at all.

Do that one task without rushing.  
Move slowly.  
Breathe deeply.  
Let it become a quiet act of patience,  
an offering of trust to God.


Even this small choice matters.  
God sees it.  
God honors it.

You are exactly where you need to be on this journey.
