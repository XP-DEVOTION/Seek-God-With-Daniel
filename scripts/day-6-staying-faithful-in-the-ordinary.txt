Welcome to Day 6 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **Staying Faithful in the Ordinary**.


Our Scripture comes from the Gospel of Luke, chapter 16, verse 10.

Listen to these words:

“Whoever is faithful in a very little is faithful also in much.”


Holiness is not built in grand moments alone.  
It grows quietly, steadily, in the ordinary things—  
the small choices,  
the daily tasks,  
the unnoticed acts of love.

God sees every one of them.  
And in His eyes, nothing faithful is ever small.

Today, you are invited to bring your attention and your heart  
to the ordinary moments of your day.


Here is today’s reflection:

Holiness grows in ordinary moments.  
Faithfulness is built through small, steady choices.


Let us pray together.  
You may say these words aloud or hold them silently:

Jesus,  
help me stay faithful in the small things today.  
Amen.


Now, a simple activity for today.

Choose one ordinary task you will do today—  
it could be making your bed,  
preparing a meal,  
answering an email,  
or listening to someone speak.

Whatever it is, do that one task with love and full attention.  
Do it slowly, carefully, as if it matters—  
because to God, it does.

Offer it quietly to Him as an act of faithfulness.


These small acts are not wasted.  
They are the hidden bricks  
in the quiet building of a faithful life.

You are seen.  
You are loved.  
Keep going.
