Welcome to Day 21—the final day of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **Sent Forth in Peace**.


Our Scripture comes from the Gospel of John, chapter 14, verse 27.

Listen to the parting gift of Jesus:

“Peace I leave with you; my peace I give to you.”


You began this journey with a simple yes.  
You have walked through simplicity and stillness,  
through dryness and surrender,  
through weakness and quiet growth.

You end not empty,  
but filled—  
filled with a peace that is not fragile,  
a clarity that is gentle,  
a purpose that is alive.

Jesus does not send you out hurried or anxious.  
He sends you out in His own peace—  
the same peace that carried Him through the cross  
and out the other side.

Today, you are invited to receive that peace fully  
and to step forward carrying it into your ordinary, beautiful life.


Here is today’s reflection:

You end these 21 days not empty but filled—  
with peace, clarity, and purpose.  
God sends you forth in His peace.



Let us pray together one last time.  
You may speak these words softly or hold them in your heart:

Jesus,  
let Your peace guide me as I go forward.  
Amen.


Now, a simple and beautiful activity to close this journey.

Stand if you can, or sit comfortably.  
Take a slow, deep breath.

If it feels right to you,  
bless yourself with the Sign of the Cross—  
or simply place your hand over your heart.

As you do, quietly receive His sending:

You are filled.  
You are loved.  
You are sent in peace.

Now step into your day—  
into your life—  
carrying that peace with you.

Well done, faithful one.  
You have completed this journey.  
God is proud of you.  
He walks with you—  
today and every day after.

Go in peace.
