Welcome to Day 17 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **You Are Precious to God**.


Our Scripture comes from the prophet Isaiah, chapter 43, verse 4.

Listen to God’s own words spoken over you:

“You are precious in my sight, and honored, and I love you.”


These are not words you have to earn.  
They are not words you receive only on your best days.  
They are words God speaks over you simply because you are His.

You are precious—not because of what you do,  
not because of how you feel,  
but because of who He is  
and because He has chosen you.

Today, you are invited to let these words settle into the places  
that doubt your worth,  
that feel unworthy,  
that carry shame or tiredness.

Hear them again:  
You are precious.  
You are honored.  
You are loved.


Here is today’s reflection:

Your worth is not earned—it is given.  
God calls you precious.


Let us pray together.  
You may speak these words softly or hold them in your heart:

Father,  
help me receive Your love without resistance.  
Amen.


Now, a gentle activity for today.

Find a quiet moment—perhaps right now—  
look into a mirror if you can,  
or simply close your eyes and place a hand over your heart.

Then say aloud, slowly and kindly,  
as if God Himself is speaking it to you:

“I am precious to God.”

Say it once, or say it several times.  
Let the words be a gift you receive,  
not a task you perform.


You do not need to feel it fully for it to be true.  
It is true because God has said it.  
And He never lies.

You are precious.  
You are honored.  
You are deeply, eternally loved.
