Welcome to Day 12 of this 21-day journey.

Today’s theme is **Purifying Intentions**.


Our Scripture comes from Psalm 51, verse 10.

Listen to the heartfelt plea of the psalmist:

“Create in me a clean heart, O God.”


Over time, our intentions can become mixed.  
We begin seeking God with a pure heart,  
but slowly other motives creep in—  
wanting to feel good,  
to appear spiritual,  
to earn approval.

God does not condemn this.  
He gently purifies.  
He brings us back, again and again,  
to the simple why behind our devotion:  
to know Him and to love Him.

Today is an invitation to let Him cleanse and renew your heart’s deepest intention.


Here is today’s reflection:

God purifies your intentions gently.  
He brings you back to the ‘why’ behind your devotion.


Let us pray together.  
You may speak these words softly or hold them in your heart:

Lord,  
purify my heart.  
Make my intentions simple and true.  
Amen.


Now, a quiet activity for today.

Find a moment of stillness—perhaps right now—  
and ask yourself this gentle question:

Why am I seeking God today?

Don’t judge the answer that comes.  
Simply notice it.  
Then, if you can, write it down—  
one honest sentence,  
or even a few words.

Offer that intention to God  
and ask Him to purify it,  
to bring it back to its truest, simplest form.


God is not looking for perfect motives.  
He is looking for honest ones  
that He can gently shape.

Your heart is safe with Him.
